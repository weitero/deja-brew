# Déjà Brew

> The noblest death a bean can die is in the grinder.

**[Déjà Brew](https://weitero.itch.io/dj-brew)** is a fast-paced, top-down arcade game set **inside an espresso machine**. You play as a sentient coffee bean navigating the cold, rhythmic interior of the machine — dodging grinding teeth, riding conveyor gears, and timing your sacrifice to the machine's relentless cycle. Inspired by *Snake* and *Pikmin*, you lead chains of fellow beans into the grinder blade, dose the portafilter, and extract the perfect shot. The machine doesn't care about you. It just grinds. Your glory is in how precisely you feed it.
